### Sony Ericsson Xperia X1 preview Review

**Reviewed by**: [Andrew Lim](http://reviews.cnet.co.uk/andrewlim/)

**Reviewed on**: 30 May 2008

Aside from being Sony Ericsson’s first Windows Mobile phone, the Xperia X1 also looks set to be one of Sony Ericsson’s most successful smart phones yet. Boasting a cornucopia of features including a full Qwerty keypad, we have high hopes for the X1.

The Sony Ericsson Xperia X1 will be available to buy in June and will most likely come free on a monthly contract.
