# Seiko SCVS013 aka Blue Spark

Now this is an interesting piece, it was very popular for a while and everybody is hunting high and low for it.

I was on the fence, I like dress watches but I didn’t have that feeling for it.. so days goes by, 1 came up for sale, I was like ok, let’s grab it first and decide later.

So viola, the Blue Spark

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8325/8104282220_bb0e79f90c_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

I can tell why it’s so popular, it has a very classic, clean feel and somehow I feel like I’m wearing a vintage Grand or King Seiko.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8330/8104283810_202367beb6_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

The white is so perfect

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8048/8104270021_c9144ed7a6_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

The blue is very subtle, yet so mesmerising.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8051/8104287476_c0a7d93ac5_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

The usual 6R15..

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8328/8104273255_22766fdcd7_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

My Seiko dress pair, the Cocktail is definitely bigger than the Blue Spark. Although the star burst dial is the most attractive feature of the Cocktail, in a static photo, your eyes will just be drawn to the Blue Spark.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8326/8104289142_5d8b29318e_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

The perfect pose.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8472/8104289474_6b34d5f2e1_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

Lastly, the size, well, if you look at the dimensions below, at 37mm, it’s a very small watch on my 7″ wrist, but if you wear it with a formal attire, the cuffs will cover up most of the hand, leaving the watch in the right proportion.

![Image](/media/A5iBKLFCcAA3vEr.jpg_large)

Blue Spark – Case diameter: 37mm, 40mm with crown, 44mm from lug to lug

Cocktail – Case diameter: 40mm, 43mm with crown, 47mm from lug to lug

In summary, I’m very pleased with this piece, the next perfect dress watch for me.
