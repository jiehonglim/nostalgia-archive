# Lock Unlock Lock Unlock

After I found the [hack](http://jiehong.org/blog/2008/10/05/modifying-microsoft-exchange-security-policy-on-windows-mobile/) to extend or remove the auto phone lock which is enforced by the Microsoft Exchange Security Policy. The changed settings don’t really last, even after disabling the “Enable Device Policy Update” !

So as long as that 15min time out lock came back, I have to apply the hack settings again. Oh bummer..
