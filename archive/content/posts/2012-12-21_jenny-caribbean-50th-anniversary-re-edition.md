# Jenny Caribbean 50th Anniversary Re-edition

When okies8 first shared this back in Nov, I was so tempted yet unsure. The yellow is screaming out at me but I was hesitant, so I waited for more photos of the actual watch, as they slowly appear on WUS, I decided to jump onto it. Placed an order by end of Nov, the watch finally arrived almost a month later.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8072/8293060046_5c7705f2a3_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

At the first glance, it’s almost exactly a replica of the original Jenny Caribbean, the distinct differences are bigger case, screwed down case back vs a monobloc case design, dial and hands and the logo used.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8073/8293061524_835ce8d917_c.jpg?resize=525%2C351)

The old and new side by side, the bezel with the decompression table is what stands out in both Jenny.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8083/8293060220_a4dc147855_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

It’s like falling in love all over again, the perfectly finished brushing on case, the bezel turns smoothly and clicks into alignment. Not a diver, so I can’t make any sense out of the decompression table along the bezel.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8491/8292007869_a45c74d5ba_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

The case back is very different with Jenny’s first logo, the Xiphias (sword fish). Quoting from Doxa announcement, “The new (old) logo is historical to JENNY watches and represents a the early days in the evolution of the Jenny Caribbean.”

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm5.staticflickr.com/4134/4889799609_c7f7cbd1fd_z.jpg?resize=525%2C395)

The original fish logo for reference, which is currently adopted in all Doxa sub watches.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8362/8293060646_fb9d3de6db_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

Signed buckle, since we are on the topic of strap, I have very mixed feelings about it, I believe other owners shared the same as well. It’s very comfortable but it picks up dust and lint like magnet. The strap have to go.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8491/8293060868_21478ffa48_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)

Signed crown with the new old logo.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8216/8292008473_4974a9f5b8_c.jpg?resize=525%2C350)
