# Aquadive Bathyscaphe 100 Review

Finally, I got some time with my camera + natural lighting after yesterday’s unboxing armed with just my iPhone 4S.

Aquadive Bathyscaphe 100

I am very impressed with the solid built of the watch, there’s definitely a lot of wrist presence with the Bathyscaphe 100.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm8.staticflickr.com/7002/6392944373_45ff7ff183_z.jpg?w=525)

The whole dial design, hour markers and the choice of hands makes it very attractive as a modern diver yet retain some of the old designs.

Continue reading 

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm8.staticflickr.com/7032/6392944697_0354098fa0_z.jpg?w=525)

The whole crown operation feels really good and solid with brushed finishing for the case.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm8.staticflickr.com/7003/6392945247_551ca2e364_z.jpg?w=525)

The white indicators for the bezel insert is something unique for me.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm8.staticflickr.com/7028/6392945821_5ff1f3c3d2_z.jpg?w=525)

Top down, it’s just beautiful.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm7.staticflickr.com/6213/6392946729_f29db94940_z.jpg?w=525)

Yes, I got #8, lucky 8.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm8.staticflickr.com/7017/6392948751_20009d946c_z.jpg?w=525)

Side by side with popular Seiko Marine Master 300 (MM300)

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm8.staticflickr.com/7173/6392947923_6e8ebaf023_z.jpg?w=525)

Both watches have the same feel and aura around it.

![Image](https://i0.wp.com/farm7.staticflickr.com/6052/6392947129_55c4e151fa_z.jpg?w=525)

Side profiles, both are hefty and wears a little higher.

Although the MM300 feature a monobloc case, the Bathyscaphe has a higher water resistance at 1000m with a HEV.

To me, they are both great watches.
