# A better, faster Win32 PDF Reader

Why use bloatware like [Adobe Reader](http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2_allversions.html) ?

Adobe Reader 7.07 – 20.3MB

Adobe Reader 6.0.1 – 15.9MB

Adobe Reader 5.05 – 9.9MB

[Foxit Reader](http://www.foxitsoftware.com/pdf/rd_intro.php) 1.3 – 1.11MB, Uncompressed exe 2.42MB

Single executable file. Faster launch times. Adobe Reader look and feel. Do the comparison.

Of course in Linux, there is always [Evince](http://www.gnome.org/projects/evince/)
