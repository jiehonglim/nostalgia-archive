# Increasing the volume of your HTC Touch Pro

Adding another tweak which helps in increasing the overall volume for HTC Touch Pro

Make a copy of the /Windows/AudioPara3.csv to your folder for backup

Unzip  the AudioPara3_lighter_boost.zip or AudioPara3_full_boost.zip and move the extracted AudioPara3.csv to /Windows/.

As it’s a system folder, you may need to use [Total Commander](http://www.ghisler.com/ce.htm) or [Resco Explorer](http://www.resco.net/pocketpc/explorer/) to execute that action.

Reset your phone for it to take effect.
