# Updated my iPhonelock

Kind author of [IPhonelock](http://www.smart-se.ru/forums/index.php?showtopic=4361), kwull has updated his software version to 0.50 with a few bugfixes and enhancements.

The iphonelock.ini has more room for tweaking especially the brightness level which can be very battery draining.

Old “bugs” that I encountered like not locking after a phone call is gone.

There are visual icons for missed calls and unread SMS on the locked screen.

For P1, now the camera button is disabled when it’s locked.

Well, seeing is believing, download the latest 0.50 version from the [forum](http://www.smart-se.ru/forums/index.php?showtopic=4361). Remember to sign it with [www.symbiansigned.com
